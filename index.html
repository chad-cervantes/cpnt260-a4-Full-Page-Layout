Meet Rebecca Atwater

Summary
My name is Rebecca Atwater, and I'm from Calgary, Alberta! I have been a professional photographer for 5 years now! I specialize in wild life, nature, and wedding photography!
Wild Life & Nature
Ever since I was a kid, I have always had a curiosity of watching nature shows! This drew me to love animals and nature. Whenever I went for a hike with friends or family, I always carried my camera with me to capture the beauty of nature and its wildlife.
Wedding
For every wedding I went to, I always wanted to capture those precious memories from that day. Weddings are those once in a lifetime events where I needed to ensure that those memories will forever be remembered.
